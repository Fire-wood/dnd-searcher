Level: 0
Casting: 1 Action
Range: 120
Components: V, S
Duration: Instantaneous
School: Evocation
Book: PHB 237
Classes: Warlock
Edition: 5
Description: A beam of crackling energy streaks toward a creature within range. Make a ranged spell attack against the target. On a hit, the target takes 1d10 force damage. 
Higher levels: The spell creates more than one beam when you reach higher levels:\nTwo beams at 5th level\nThree beams at 11th level\nFour beams at 17th level.\nYou can direct the beams at the same target or at different ones. Make a separate attack roll for each beam.
